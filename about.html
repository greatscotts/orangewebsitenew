ORIGIN

We started building khawna last January 2014 because all of us believed on this product and we all came together for one reason - "to change the way on how the world learns". Our founder Gian Javelona started OrangeApps Inc. when he was still a student of PUP, building its first product, the PUP mobile portal the said portal got the attention of the start up scene in the Philippines. The company flourished through the help of the leading incubator in the Philippines - Ideaspace Foundation and also got its seed round of funding from other Angel investors in the country.
